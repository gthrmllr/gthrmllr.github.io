About us :

Perfectly situated at the foot of the Vosges mountains, you will also have the opportunity to benefit from the surrounding activities that make the region famous: the Kruth lake (adventure park), the Grand Ballon, a number of nearby ski resorts (Markstein, La Bresse, Ventron...), a paragliding school, the start of the wine route (Thann and its famous Rangen vineyards), as well as numerous museums and castles... without forgetting the traditional Alsatian Christmas markets.

On the spot, you will be close to numerous bycicle and pedestrian hikes starting from the house. Mulhouse is located 35 minutes away, Colmar 45 minutes and Europapark 1 hour and 30 minutes away. In Cernay you will find indoor activities to do with friends or family (cinema, karting, indoor surfing, bowling). The swimming pool, open all year round, is located 200 meters from the house.

The cottage is in the center of the Wesserling park in the shade of the lime trees, a calm and quiet place that allows you to thoroughly enjoy the time you spend in these remarkable gardens.

The apartment, with a surface area of approximately 140m2, can accommodate 6 to 8 people. It has 2 double bedrooms, one bedroom with a twin bed, a baby bed and a sofa bed. It has a fully equipped kitchen, a large living room and a dining room. The bathroom has a shower, a balneotherapy bathtub and a sauna. The toilets are separate. You will have the use of a part of the of the garden with its barbecue. You will also have access to games for children, and bicycles can be lent to you. For a few days or more, we hope you will enjoy your stay.
